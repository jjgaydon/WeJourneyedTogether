Post-Encounter Scene

The Main Player of the Encounter rolls an Aspect Hand. They then resolve a Scene based on the options available:

• Relax or Reflect (2 of that Aspect): Choose another Traveler to converse with over down time. Roleplay how this transpires. You gain an Experience Point.

• Train Another (3 of that Aspect): Choose another Traveler and offer to help train them in this Aspect. Roleplay how this transpires. They gain +1 Maturity in this Aspect.

• Train Self (4 of that Aspect): Choose another Traveler to provide guidance or aid in your Traveler training in this Aspect. Roleplay how this transpires. You gain +1 Maturity in this Aspect.

• Heal Another (3 of that Aspect): Choose another Traveler and attempt to help them deal with a Wound in this Aspect. Roleplay how this transpires. They may Resolve the Wound.

• Heal Self (4 of that Aspect): Choose another Traveler and attempt to ask for their help in dealing with a Wound in this Aspect. Roleplay how this transpires. You may Resolve the Wound.

At the end of a Scene, any player may spend 4 Experience Points to resolve a Wound. Then, increase all Wounds.
